Great food! Spectacular service! Classy ambiance!

Well what can I say, was recommended by a friend to visit this place. Turned out to be one of the best wine and dine experiences of my life. They take their multicuisine speciality seriously! The Caesar salad was impeccably plated and tasted like Italian heaven! For the mains I decided to go Mughlai with the mutton rara coupled with some garlic naans complimented with steamed rice 🤤. Would definitely visit again. What really took the experience up a notch was the service! Whether you're going alone, as a couple or with family/ friends, the service will definitely elevate your experience. Felt like dining at an upper upscale hotel!
